The Best Movies According to Karen

My Top 3 Movies of all time

Trainspotting

Timeless classic, capturing a key moment in history and propelling Ewan McGregor into world wide stardom. Superb performance from Robert Caryle as the infamous Begbie. "Its shite being Scottish"

Mamma Mia

All your favourite Abba hits incorporated into an unorthodox love story. Meryl Streep is excellent but Pierce Brosnan's signing is questionable!

The Lion King

Ultimate Disney classic with unbelievable music. I defy you to not shed a tear during this masterpiece.
